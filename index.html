August

You walked me home after but it felt like an attack.

The air was slick with humidity, the correlation between concreteness and variance. It was just data.

You asked me what my concerns were. Can' t you know time is a graveyard of teeth? There' s no space to simply rest and enjoy running.

The world will hang the troubled fool in front of them.

You never made me feel like you. All this instead of the branch. The light fading.

It was much safer this way.
